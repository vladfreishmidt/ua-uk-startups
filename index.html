Ukrainian-UK Tech Start Up Competition

The aim of this initiative is to bring together the tech startup communities of the UK and Ukraine to share knowledge, expertise and create new business opportunities

We’re pleased to be supporting this initiative that brings together two of Europe’s leading tech nations. The UK and Ukraine share a passion for entrepreneurship in the digital economy, and it’s a great opportunity for bright new talent from Ukraine to showcase their businesses in London.
Natalia Galibarenko, Ambassador of Ukraine to the UK

Goals

  • Highlight the growing startup ecosystem in Ukraine and to broaden avenues for business collaboration with the UK
  • Provide the winning Ukrainian startups with a bespoke and focused series of introductions to business partners, investors and potential clients in the UK
  • Host a bespoke MiniBar event (one of the biggest startup events in London) to give the startups an opportunity to pitch their services/products to a wider audience

Timing

  • Launch1st of April 2019 Kyiv
  • Application deadline28th of April 12pm
  • Judging event and Winners announced15th of May
  • UK Business Acceleration ProgramJune

Competition Prize - Take Part in ‘Ukrainian Tech Tour UK’

  • Flight to London - Five winners will be flown over to London in June to join London Tech Week
  • Paid accommodation for three nights
  • Take part in a bespoke startup tour to make new business contacts and showcase to the UK startup ecosystem
  • Showcase at the biggest startup Meetup in London - MiniBar
  • Promote and showcase their businesses as part of Ukrainian Pavilion at London Tech Week

The Ukrainian startup tour will take 4 days during which the winning Ukrainian startups will participate in different events and individual introductions so they get maximum value.
